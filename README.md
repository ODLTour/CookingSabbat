# CookingSabbat
Become a witch to make the ghosts' wish come true!
You are the only one to allow them to enter the world of the living.
At dusk, they knock at your manor's door asking for your magical beverage. With this potion, they become at last able to play tricks to humans and scare children.
 
Enter an immersive virtual reality and start to prepare the potion in your cauldron. 
With a VR headset and hands' controllers, you grab ingredients, stir them with a ladle and end up having the magical liquid the ghosts are waiting for. Each interaction is natural as a physical action. Watch and listen for immediate visual and auditory feedback to discover and master new recipes.
 
Be careful! Each ghost has a specific need and limited patience. Should you prepare an unsuitable potion or need more time than he gives you , this ghost will put a spell on you: the darkness will invade your kitchen and you won't be able to cook properly and to make progress in the gaming.
 
With its esthetical universe and its meticulously designed soundscape, Cooking Sabbat is designed to avoid cognitive overload. You stay focused on the cooking experience. Such as a beginner with an intuitive play process or as an experimented witch with rewarded challenges.
 
Let's the ghosts enter and the Cooking Sabbat experience begin !
